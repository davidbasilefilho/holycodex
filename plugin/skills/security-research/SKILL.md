name: security-research
description: Evidence-led security research without team mode.
---

Main agent owns scope, threat surface, dedupe, PoC, severity, report. At most two useful lanes: explorer internal, librarian external. Require attack path before severity. Require safe local PoC or static proof for high or critical. Separate CWE and severity. Speculative hardening is not finding. Report surviving, downgraded, rejected findings, evidence, commands, minimal fixes, residual risk. Never attack real or third-party systems.
